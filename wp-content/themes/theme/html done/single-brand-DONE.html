Comfort, Coziness, Elegance: Cozmo Home

Facebook-square Instagram Twitter

For a personalized shopping experience, Cozmo Home brings together a wide range of kitchen supplies, tableware, and home accessories from customers’ favourite brands such as Irfan, Abdeen, Samiz, and Whole and All.

Cozmo Home was introduced to the Jordanian market in early 2019 and offers a variety of home accessories and appliances, conveniently located at the 7th Circle Cozmo Centre.

About THE Group

THE Group is one of Jordan’s leading retail companies, established in 2003. We are committed to providing our shoppers with exceptional customer service and unparalleled quality. THE Group owns and operates the leading supermarket chain known as Cozmo and it has the franchise for the UK-based world’s largest toy shop; Hamleys, in the Levant area which includes Egypt, Lebanon, Syria, Iraq, Palestine, and Jordan.
