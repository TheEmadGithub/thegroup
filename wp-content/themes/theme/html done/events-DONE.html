About THE Group

THE Group is one of Jordan’s leading retail companies, established in 2003. We are committed to providing our shoppers with exceptional customer service and unparalleled quality. THE Group owns and operates the leading supermarket chain known as Cozmo and it has the franchise for the UK-based world’s largest toy shop; Hamleys, in the Levant area which includes Egypt, Lebanon, Syria, Iraq, Palestine, and Jordan.
