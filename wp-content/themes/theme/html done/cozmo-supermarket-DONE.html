With the aim of bringing something new to the Jordanian market, Cozmo opened its doors in 2003.

Growing as the leading supermarket in Amman, Cozmo caters to the needs of customers with a large variety of local and imported brands that meet the continuously increasing demands of its customers.

With an overall store size of 4,800 m2, the supermarket’s departments include a bakery, butchery, fresh produce section, deli, frozen food, healthy food, and all other general products needed for everyday use.

THE Bakery

Our Bakery offers the best selection of delicious Mediterranean and Western breads, sweets and pastries, made fresh daily. Our customizable cakes made by our master bakers are perfect for any event or special occasion.

THE Butchery

Find fresh seafood, chicken and meat from our butchery, or select one of our prime cuts of imported US, Australian and South African beef. Also available are a variety of pre-marinated, ready-to-cook, chicken and meat, perfect for weeknight meals.

THE Deli

Flavours from all over the globe can be found in our deli. We offer a wide selection of cheeses and cold cut deli meats imported from Italy, the UK, the Netherlands, Switzerland, Spain and more!

Fresh Produce

Our fresh local and imported fruits and vegetables are available year-round in addition to a diverse selection of seasonal produce.

A gourmet supermarket serving the local communities! 

Simply Cozmo was an expansion project commenced by THE Group in 2010 as a convenient neighborhood store, with 4 branches in West Amman, it offers a customized shopping experience answering your everyday needs.

The Kingdom’s first supermarket e-commerce website was launched in 2016, giving customers the freedom to shop from the convenience and comfort of their home or office.

With a seamless shopping experience, customers can choose from an extensive list of items, select a time for delivery within a 24-hour window.
